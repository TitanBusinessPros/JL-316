Expert Tips for Oklahoma Lawn Care

Durant falls in USDA hardiness zone 7b, where Bermudagrass, Zoysia, and tall fescue thrive. Our specialists recommend mowing at the correct height (2.5–3 inches for fescue, 1–2 inches for Bermuda) to shade out weeds. During summer, deep watering twice a week beats daily sprinkling. We also provide organic mulch installation to retain soil moisture and give planting beds a polished look. Whether you need drought-resistant xeriscaping or vibrant annual color rotation, J's Landscaping brings decades of local expertise to every project.
